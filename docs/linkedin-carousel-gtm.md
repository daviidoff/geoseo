## Visual Design Notes

**Colors to use:**
- Primary gradient: Blue (#3B82F6) → Purple (#8B5CF6)
- Background: Dark (#0F172A) or Light (#FFFFFF)
- Accent: Green for "unlimited" (#22C55E)
- Warning/competitor: Orange/Red (#F97316, #EF4444)

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: Bold, clean sans-serif (Inter, Geist)
- Body: Medium weight, high readability

**Icons:**
- ✅ for HyperNiche features
- ❌ for competitor pain points
- 💰 for pricing
- ♾️ for unlimited (infinity symbol)

## Posting Strategy

**Best times for LinkedIn (founder/tech audience):**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am local time
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)

**Engagement tactics:**
1. Reply to every comment in first 2 hours
2. Tag relevant people who might reshare
3. Cross-post teaser to X/Twitter
4. Follow up post 2-3 days later with results/learnings

**Hashtags (use sparingly, 3-5 max):**
#AEO #ContentMarketing #AI #Startup #SEO
